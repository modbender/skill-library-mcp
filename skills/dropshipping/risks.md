# Dropshipping Risk Assessment

## Legal & Compliance Risks

### Tax Obligations

| Risk | Severity | Prevention |
|------|----------|------------|
| EU VAT/OSS | 🔴 HIGH | Register OSS if >€10k sales to EU consumers. Fines up to 200% of unpaid VAT |
| US Sales Tax Nexus | 🔴 HIGH | Use TaxJar/Avalara. Monitor sales by state. Thresholds: typically $100k or 200 transactions |
| Customs/Duties | 🟠 MEDIUM | Declare real values. Warn customers about potential charges. Use local warehouses |

### Consumer Protection (EU)

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Right of Withdrawal | 14 days to return, no questions asked. Mandatory. |
| Legal Warranty | 2 years. If product fails, customer can demand repair/replacement/refund |
| Pre-contractual Info | Must show: total price, shipping costs, seller identity, conditions |

### Intellectual Property

| Risk | Severity | Prevention |
|------|----------|------------|
| Counterfeit Products | 🔴 CRITICAL | NEVER sell branded products without authorization. Verify authenticity |
| Patent Infringement | 🔴 HIGH | Search patents before listing "innovative" products from China |
| Copyright (Images) | 🟠 MEDIUM | Use your own photos or get explicit license |

## Financial Risks

### Chargebacks

| Threshold | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| <0.5% | Safe zone |
| 0.5-1% | Warning from processor |
| >1% | Account review, potential restrictions |
| >2% | Account closure, funds held |

**Prevention:**
- 3D Secure for all transactions
- Verify AVS (address verification)
- Reject suspicious orders (mismatched billing/shipping, disposable emails)
- Provide tracking ALWAYS
- Proactive communication reduces disputes 40%
- Refund before dispute when appropriate

### Cash Flow Traps

| Risk | Prevention |
|------|------------|
| PayPal 90-day hold | Diversify processors. Don't depend only on PayPal |
| Negative cycle (pay supplier before receiving payment) | Maintain 3 months operating capital |
| Accumulated returns | Provision 10-15% for refunds in accounting |

### Real Margin Calculation

```
ACTUAL costs to include:
├── Product cost
├── Supplier shipping
├── Payment processor fees (3-4%)
├── Platform fees (if applicable): 5-15%
├── Advertising (CAC): varies widely
├── Returns/refunds (10-15%)
├── Chargebacks (1-2%)
├── Customer service: $0.50-2 per order
├── Software/apps
└── Taxes on profit

If margin < 15% after ALL costs → DANGER ZONE
```

## Operational Risks

### Supplier Problems

| Risk | Severity | Prevention |
|------|----------|------------|
| Supplier disappears | 🔴 HIGH | 2-3 backup suppliers per product |
| Quality varies | 🔴 HIGH | Periodic test orders, monitor customer photos |
| Price changes | 🟠 MEDIUM | Written agreements, safety margin in pricing |
| Ghost stock | 🟠 MEDIUM | Verify stock before promising delivery |

### Shipping

| Issue | Impact | Prevention |
|-------|--------|------------|
| 15-30 day shipping | Cancellations, chargebacks | Be BRUTALLY honest: "2-4 weeks" visible BEFORE purchase |
| Customs delays | Additional 2-4 weeks | Correct declaration, communicate proactively |
| Peak seasons | 30-60 day delays | Pause ads, use local warehouses, checkout warnings |

### Quality Control

| Risk | Prevention |
|------|------------|
| 5-20% defect rate from AliExpress | Factor into margins, clear replacement policy |
| Toxic materials | Require CE, CPSC certifications. Don't sell children's products without verification |
| Product differs from photos | Use YOUR OWN photos, accurate descriptions with measurements |

## Reputational Risks

### Platform Suspensions

| Platform | Common Causes | Recovery |
|----------|---------------|----------|
| Amazon | IP claims, metrics, restricted products | Plan of Action required, 50% don't recover |
| Shopify | Chargebacks, fraud, prohibited products | Clean record essential, if banned start fresh |
| PayPal | Disputes, suspicious activity | Hard to appeal, 180 days to recover funds |
| Facebook Ads | Policy violations, negative feedback | Multiple Business Managers recommended |

### Reviews Impact

| Platform | Effect of Negative Reviews |
|----------|---------------------------|
| Amazon | <4 stars = 80% visibility drop, <3.5 = death |
| Google/Trustpilot | Affects 90% of purchase decisions |
| Social Media | One viral complaint = business end |

## Red Flags — STOP IMMEDIATELY IF:

❌ Real margin <15% after all costs
❌ Supplier doesn't respond within 48h
❌ No tracking available
❌ Product has unverifiable health claims
❌ Product similar to known brand (IP risk)
❌ No way to verify quality
❌ Shipping time >21 days without local warehouse
❌ Supplier asks for payment outside platform
❌ Supplier rating <95%
❌ No clear return policy from supplier

## Minimum Compliance Checklist

### Before Launch
- [ ] Legal entity formed (LLC, SL, etc.)
- [ ] VAT/OSS registration if selling to EU
- [ ] Separate business bank account
- [ ] 2+ payment processors configured
- [ ] Policies: privacy, returns, terms
- [ ] Liability insurance
- [ ] Verified supplier with samples received
- [ ] Tracking confirmed for all shipments
- [ ] Functional customer service system
- [ ] 3 months operating capital

### Monthly
- [ ] Review chargeback ratio
- [ ] Quality audit with test orders
- [ ] Verify tax compliance
- [ ] Analyze real margin per product
- [ ] Customer data backup
- [ ] Review and respond to reviews

### When Problems Arise
- [ ] Document EVERYTHING
- [ ] Respond within 24h
- [ ] Refund before dispute if possible
- [ ] Escalate to lawyer if legal threat
- [ ] Have exit plan if metrics degrade
