# 名人名言清單

根據 `day_of_year % 20 + 1` 選擇。

## 科技/創業類

1. **Steve Jobs**: "Stay hungry, stay foolish."
   - 求知若飢，虛心若愚。

2. **Steve Jobs**: "The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do."
   - 那些瘋狂到認為自己可以改變世界的人，才是真正改變世界的人。

3. **Elon Musk**: "When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor."
   - 當一件事夠重要時，即使勝算不大，你也會去做。

4. **Jeff Bezos**: "In the end, we are our choices."
   - 最終，我們就是我們所做的選擇。

5. **Bill Gates**: "It's fine to celebrate success, but it is more important to heed the lessons of failure."
   - 慶祝成功是好的，但更重要的是從失敗中學習。

6. **Mark Zuckerberg**: "The biggest risk is not taking any risk."
   - 最大的風險就是不冒任何風險。

7. **Reid Hoffman**: "If you are not embarrassed by the first version of your product, you've launched too late."
   - 如果你不為產品的第一版感到尷尬，那你發布得太晚了。

8. **Peter Thiel**: "Competition is for losers."
   - 競爭是輸家的遊戲。

## 投資/智慧類

9. **Ray Dalio**: "Pain plus reflection equals progress."
   - 痛苦加上反思等於進步。

10. **Ray Dalio**: "He who lives by the crystal ball will eat shattered glass."
    - 靠水晶球預測未來的人，終將吞下碎玻璃。

11. **Warren Buffett**: "The stock market is designed to transfer money from the active to the patient."
    - 股市的設計就是把錢從急躁的人轉移到有耐心的人手中。

12. **Warren Buffett**: "Risk comes from not knowing what you're doing."
    - 風險來自於不知道自己在做什麼。

13. **Charlie Munger**: "The best thing a human being can do is to help another human being know more."
    - 人能做的最好的事，就是幫助另一個人知道更多。

14. **Naval Ravikant**: "Seek wealth, not money or status."
    - 追求財富，而非金錢或地位。

## 領導/人生類

15. **Winston Churchill**: "Success is not final, failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts."
    - 成功不是終點，失敗也不是致命的，重要的是繼續前進的勇氣。

16. **Nelson Mandela**: "It always seems impossible until it's done."
    - 在完成之前，一切總看起來不可能。

17. **Albert Einstein**: "Imagination is more important than knowledge."
    - 想像力比知識更重要。

18. **Mahatma Gandhi**: "Be the change you wish to see in the world."
    - 成為你想在世界上看到的改變。

19. **Martin Luther King Jr.**: "The time is always right to do what is right."
    - 做對的事情，永遠是對的時機。

20. **Theodore Roosevelt**: "Do what you can, with what you have, where you are."
    - 用你所擁有的，在你所在的地方，做你能做的事。
