## What's Inside

- **Strategic preparation system** — Negotiation brief template, counterpart profiling, power analysis matrix
- **7-phase workflow** — From research through closing and post-negotiation review
- **Core techniques** — Mirroring, labeling, calibrated questions, accusation audit, Ackerman method, tactical silence
- **4 complete playbooks** — Salary, vendor, client deal, and partnership negotiation with word-for-word scripts
- **Body language & verbal tells guide** — Read the room in person, on video, or over email
- **Advanced tactics** — Multi-party negotiations, cross-cultural considerations, handling difficult counterparts
- **Scoring rubric** — Rate your negotiation performance across 6 weighted dimensions
- **Post-negotiation review template** — Capture lessons and improve over time
