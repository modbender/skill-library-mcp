# Software License Manager

Audit, track, and optimize software licenses across your organization. Catches waste, flags compliance risks, and preps vendor negotiations.

## Why

Average company wastes 25-30% of SaaS spend on unused seats and forgotten subscriptions. A proper audit recovers $1,000-$3,000 per employee per year.

## What You Get

- **Full license inventory** — SaaS, perpetual, usage-based, open-source, enterprise agreements
- **Waste detection** — ghost seats, duplicate tools, tier bloat, orphan licenses
- **Compliance scanner** — expired licenses, exceeded seats, GPL/AGPL risks
- **12-month renewal calendar** — with 60/30/15-day alerts
- **Negotiation scorecards** — usage data, alternatives, leverage points, walk-away prices
- **Cost optimization roadmap** — quick wins, medium-term consolidation, strategic plays
