## What You Get

- **Decision classification** — Type 1-4 system so you don't over-analyze two-way doors or rush one-way doors
- **10 decision frameworks** — Weighted matrix, second-order thinking, inversion, regret minimization, opportunity cost, RICE, Bayesian updating, scenario planning, expected value, kill criteria
- **15-bias checklist** — Catch confirmation bias, anchoring, sunk cost, and 12 more before they wreck your decision

- **Decision policies** — Turn recurring decisions into rules so you only decide once
- **100-point quality rubric** — Score your decision process, not just outcomes
- **Speed guide** — Match analysis depth to decision stakes automatically
