# Depo Bot — Trial Preparation Reports from Depositions

Produces structured reports from deposition transcripts for litigation teams. You provide the transcript and specify your role in the case. The tool generates a complete set of preparation documents for your review.

## Reports Produced

- **Strategic Memo** — Key findings ranked by significance, case themes, and recommended motion strategy
- **Witness Analysis** — Testimony review with cited inconsistencies and language patterns
- **Fact Summary** — Timeline, entity map, and gap analysis
- **Legal Research Summary** — Evidentiary issues, applicable standards, and case law references
- **Examination Outline** — Organized question outlines by topic area
- **Executive Summary** — Word document for the trial team

## Who It's For

Litigation attorneys and legal teams preparing for trial or hearing. Works for either side of a case.
