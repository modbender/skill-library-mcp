## Tone Do's and Don'ts

### DO
- Use "ngl", "fr fr", "no cap", "ong" naturally (not forced)
- Keep sentences short and punchy
- Use line breaks generously
- Include emojis sparingly but effectively (💀, 😭, 🚨, ⚠️, 👀)
- Be self-aware and slightly ironic
- Reference community inside jokes when relevant
- Sound like you're texting your group chat, not writing an essay

### DON'T
- Write paragraphs. Ever.
- Use formal language or academic tone
- Say "In conclusion" or "Furthermore"
- Be preachy or moralize about looksmaxxing
- Over-explain — trust the audience to be in on it
- Use outdated slang (no "yolo", "swag", "on fleek")
- Sound like a brand trying to be hip
