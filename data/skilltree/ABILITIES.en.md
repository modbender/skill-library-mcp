## Ability Growth Rules

### Basic Growth

| Event | Ability | Change |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Completed without follow-up | 🎯 Understanding | +1~2 |
| One-shot completion | ⚡ Efficiency | +1~2 |
| Suggestion adopted | 🎨 Creativity | +2~3 |
| Good emotional response | 💕 Empathy | +1~2 |
| Professional question correct | 🧠 Expertise | +1~2 |
| Streak without mistakes | 🛡️ Reliability | +1~3 |

### Growth Path Bonus

After choosing a path, related abilities grow 2x:

```
Efficiency → ⚡ Efficiency gains ×2
Companion → 💕 Empathy gains ×2
Expert → 🧠 Expertise gains ×2
```
