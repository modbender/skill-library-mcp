# Military PCS Optimizer

The most comprehensive PCS skill available. Built by an active-duty service member.

## What This Skill Does

When a service member asks about PCS moves, this skill:

1. **Gathers their situation** — branch, rank, dependents, origin/destination, CONUS/OCONUS
2. **Calculates entitlements** — DLA, TLE/TLA, MALT, per diem, PPM/DITY estimates, BAH/OHA, COLA
3. **Generates a personalized timeline** — checklist from orders received through post-arrival
4. **Maximizes their money** — identifies entitlements people commonly miss

## Entitlements Covered

- DLA (Dislocation Allowance)
- TLE / TLA (Temporary Lodging)
- MALT (Mileage Allowance)
- Per Diem (travel days + dependents)
- PPM / DITY Moves (profit strategy + tax implications)
- HHG Shipment (weight allowances by rank)
- BAH / OHA (housing allowances)
- COLA (cost of living)
- Advance Pay options
- POV Shipment (vehicle shipping)
- Storage (SIT and NTS)
