## Choosing Guide

**Stay in Funchal if:**
- First time visiting
- Don't want to rent a car
- Like restaurants and nightlife
- Working remotely with need for coworking

**Stay outside Funchal if:**
- Seeking quiet and nature
- Have a car (essential)
- Want better value
- Already know the island

**North coast if:**
- Want dramatic scenery daily
- Don't mind rain/clouds
- Seeking solitude
- Escaping for creative work

See individual zone files for detailed guidance.
